The Heatmatrix.

Each year, the Heatmatrix is a bird's eye to technology disruption. Every cell crosses one of five technology families with one of ten industries, its intensity driven by hard data (investment, unicorns, startups, patents and publications) and field intelligence from 600+ reports, 1,000 professionals surveyed and expert interviews. The result is an overview of where disruption is gaining traction, where it is accelerating, and where blind spots may lie. Hover over any cell to explore the insight behind it.
